feat-2025-087 ✦ Frozen ↳ spawned 1 child
Dietary Restriction Menu Filtering
🏥 Hospital Food Ordering Jordan • 2025-09-14 9/9 stages
Provenance Chain
P
Problem
Patients with dietary restrictions are being shown menu items they cannot safely consume. Post-surgery patients, allergy patients, and those with medication interactions are unable to distinguish safe from unsafe items.
A
Analysis
Three patient types identified with overlapping restriction categories. Edge case: patients with multiple simultaneous restrictions (e.g., dairy + high-sodium + post-surgery protocol) require AND logic, not OR logic on filtering.
Decision
Chose AND-combination filtering over simple category exclusion after client demo revealed a patient could have 3+ simultaneous restriction types. OR logic would have shown too many items incorrectly.
R
Requirements
Filter applies at menu-load time, not per-item. Must handle: dietary type, allergy category, medication protocol, and post-surgery window. Items outside scope: portion size restrictions (separate feature).
···
+ 6 more stages (Design, Implementation, Validation, Documentation, Delivery, Support)
Evolved → feat-2026-012
Spawn reason: Remove deprecated restriction type CLASS_3, simplify filter logic. Original restriction type was retired by client in Q4 2025.
